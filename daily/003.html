They reportedly needed dozens of takes to get one perfect run. The falling piano hits the chord on the beat.

The story

Five hundred years ago, Leonardo drew a man inside a circle and a square and quietly made the argument we’re still catching up to: art and engineering are not different jobs. They’re one discipline with two reputations.

The marble machine isn’t a gadget that happens to make music — the building of it was musical thinking, in wood and steel. The Rube Goldberg machine isn’t a music video gimmick — it’s four minutes of physics rehearsed like choreography. Craft is what happens when making something work and making something beautiful turn out to be the same problem.

Whatever you’re building this week — code, a deck, dinner — there’s a version of it that sings. It usually costs one more crank of the handle.
