RHET 210 — Epistemic Self-Defense

The Two Registers

  • Catalog register: Logic, statistical reasoning, cognitive bias, media literacy.
  • Teaching frame: Propaganda, information warfare, and the engineered feed — how to know what’s true when the environment is adversarial.

Outcomes Served

What This Course Is About

Your information environment is built by people who want things from you. Logic, statistics, bias, and propaganda analysis — the martial art of knowing what’s true.

The Made Thing

A forensic teardown of a real piece of persuasion — its claims, techniques, and the truth of the matter, documented.
