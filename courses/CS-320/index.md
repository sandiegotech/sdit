# CS 320 — Human-AI Collaboration as Craft

**Computation & Intelligent Systems** · 4 units · Required core — everyone takes it

## The Two Registers

- **Catalog register:** Advanced practicum in human-AI collaboration — delegation, verification, evaluation, taste.
- **Teaching frame:** The studied practice of working with machine intelligence — the craft the whole age runs on.

## Outcomes Served

- **Command of Intelligence**
- **Judgment**
- **Craft**

## What This Course Is About

By Year 4 you have used these tools everywhere; now the practice itself becomes the subject. Delegation, verification, and taste — studied, drilled, and graded.

## The Made Thing

A substantial work produced with AI as instrument — with a full account of what was delegated, what was verified, and why the judgment calls were yours.
