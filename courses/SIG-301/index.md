# SIG 301 — How Scenes Form

**Culture & Signal** · 1 units · Required core — everyone takes it

## The Two Registers

- **Catalog register:** Sociology of cultural movements — subcultures, scenes, and innovation diffusion.
- **Teaching frame:** Subcultures as R&D labs: punk, hip-hop, skate, surf, internet scenes — how small groups invent what everyone uses later.

## Outcomes Served

- **Breadth of Mind**
- **Judgment**

## What This Course Is About

Every technology and art form you love was invented by a small scene before it was an industry. How those rooms work, studied closely.

## The Made Thing

A field study of a living scene — documented from inside.
