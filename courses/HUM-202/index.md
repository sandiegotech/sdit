# HUM 202 — The Industrial Mind

**The Canon** · 2 units · Required core — everyone takes it

## The Two Registers

- **Catalog register:** Nineteenth-century thought — Marx, Darwin, Nietzsche, Shelley.
- **Teaching frame:** Frankenstein + Blade Runner + the birth of electronic music. The century that invented the machine and the anxiety about it.

## Outcomes Served

- **Judgment**
- **Articulation**
- **Breadth of Mind**

## What This Course Is About

The century that built the factory, the theory of evolution, and the first fears about what we make. Shelley's question — what do you owe the thing you created? — is now an engineering requirement.
