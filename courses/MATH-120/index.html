The Two Registers

  • Catalog register: Integral calculus and series — integration, applications, infinite series, Fourier introduction.
  • Teaching frame: Sound: harmonics and Fourier through synthesizers and the studio.

Outcomes Served

What This Course Is About

Integration and infinite series, taught through the studio: every sound you have ever loved is a sum of sines, and the synthesizer is the lab bench where you prove it.

The Made Thing

A sound built from first principles — a patch or piece whose harmonic structure you can derive and defend.
