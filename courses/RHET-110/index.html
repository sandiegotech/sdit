The Two Registers

  • Catalog register: College writing — argument, evidence, structure, revision.
  • Teaching frame: Heavy output, line-edited. You write constantly; everything comes back marked.

Outcomes Served

What This Course Is About

Writing is thinking made auditable. Heavy output, line-edited, every week — because clarity on the page is clarity in the head, and both are trained the same way: by revision.

The Made Thing

A portfolio of revised essays — first drafts and finals side by side, so the distance is visible.
