About SDIT

What this is, what the program is for, and how to take part.

San Diego Institute of Technology is building an open curriculum that is structured like a serious academic program, but published openly so anyone can read it, study it, improve it, and help shape it.

The institute

What SDIT is

SDIT is the institutional home for this curriculum. The goal is to organize programs, courses, schedules, and supporting knowledge in one place so the work feels coherent, credible, and easy to navigate.

Read the knowledge base

The program

What this degree is trying to do

The main program is a four-year Bachelor of Liberal Arts built around semester volumes, chapter arcs, and daily lessons. It is designed to make serious study easier to follow, one day at a time, without losing the larger academic structure.

Contributing

How to contribute

The curriculum is open source. You can review the repository, suggest edits, expand content, improve organization, or help develop future volumes and supporting materials.
