San Diego Institute of Technology (SDIT)

Nonprofit educational and research institute (pre-BPPE, pre-accreditation; Layers 1-2 operating legally now)
premise
Industrial-era education produced specialists for a world of stable professions; that world is ending. SDIT produces people whose value compounds as machine intelligence advances — judgment, taste, epistemic self-defense, command of intelligent systems, and the craft instinct to make real things in the physical world.
thesis
The Post-Industrial Renaissance: technology, art, craft, science, and philosophy are one discipline, artificially divided by the industrial university. SDIT reunites them.
