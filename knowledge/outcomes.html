assessment

annual_gates
when
end of each year
form
portfolio review + written and oral examination against all five outcomes
unassisted_examinations
rule
Periodic unassisted examinations in writing, mathematics, and code. AI tools are open and studied everywhere — and the institution certifies the human, so it must regularly observe the human unaided.
primary_artifact
the portfolio (grades exist because transcripts require them)
capstone
CAP 401-402 defended original work — research thesis, an engineered system that works, or a venture that exists — public defense before faculty and outside examiners; the terminal demonstration of all five outcomes.
