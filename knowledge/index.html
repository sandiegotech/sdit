Knowledge Base

Canonical YAML maps for identity, principles, programs, research, and more.

  • Strategic Anchors — Enduring pillars that guide SDIT’s decisions and messaging.
  • The Catalog — Single source of truth for every course in the catalog of the BA in Technology (one degree; a concentration — Media, Design, or Engineering — declared at the end of Year 2). scripts/scaffold_catalog.py generates courses//index.md templates from this file (skipping any that already exist); after scaffolding, the markdown files are the live content source and this registry tracks structure & status.
  • Community — Fellowship programs, training rhythms, and affinity groups.
  • Degree Architecture — Bachelor of Arts in Technology — The machine-readable contract for the SDIT Bachelor of Arts in Technology: one degree, 120 semester units across eleven threads, a shared two-year Foundation, a concentration (Media, Design, or Engineering) declared at the end of Year 2, the required Crossing, annual portfolio gates against the five institutional outcomes, and a defended original work at the end.
  • Identity — Canonical organization identity (name, mission, premise, model, positioning).
  • Infrastructure — Operational footprint across digital, legal/financial, and physical domains.
  • The Five Outcomes — Assessment Framework — The five capacities SDIT certifies, assessed at annual gates: Judgment, Command of Intelligence, Craft, Articulation, Breadth of Mind. Includes the thread-to-outcome map for the Bachelor of Arts in Technology.
  • Core Philosophies — Educational and cultural principles that shape programs, research, and voice.
  • The Standing Order of Operations — The founding sequence from the SDIT Bible §VIII — in order, in public, with proof at each step.
  • Programs — The three layers of an SDIT education.
  • Active Projects — Cross-cutting project list spanning education, research, and culture.
  • Research — Research divisions and project records with status and ownership.
