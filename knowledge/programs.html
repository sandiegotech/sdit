    description
    The degree. 120 semester units, 8 semesters, one program. Years 1-2 are the Foundation, identical for every student — a common language and framework. At the end of Year 2 each student declares a concentration, chosen from experience: Media (things people watch, hear, and feel), Design (things people use), or Engineering (things that work on their own). Four depth studios in the concentration plus the Crossing — one studio from outside it, required. Field Term, defended capstone, Physical Culture throughout.
