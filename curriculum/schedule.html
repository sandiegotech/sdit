The Shape
A semester

15 weeks, five courses — the same five courses all semester, so each one builds day over day.

A week

Five days, one course per day. Monday is always the books, Friday is always the Workshop — the rhythm below never changes.

A day

One sitting, about ten minutes: one thing to experience, one idea, one question to carry. Then you're done.

Your pace

"Tomorrow" means whenever you come back. The schedule is a shape, not a clock — the site remembers where you left off on this device.
